All the admissions are strictly as per government rules & regulationsn

B.Tech.

Admissions to the institute in B.Tech. courses are on the basis of JEE (Mains) score through counseling conducted by Directorate of Technical Education, M.P. Bhopal. Remaining vacant seats if any are filled on 10+2 basis as per government rules

B.Tech. Lateral Entry (in IInd Yr.)

Direct admission in B.Tech. 2nd year (Lateral Entry) are on the basis of Diploma in Engineering from AICTE recognized institution with minimum 45% marks (40% marks in case of candidates belonging to SC/ST/OBC (excluding creamy layer) category of Madhya Pradesh)

M.C.A.

A candidate seeking admission to MCA Course should be passed- BCA / Bachelor Degree in Computer Science Engineering or equivalent Degree

M.Tech. (IE&M)

A candidate seeking admission to M.E. course should be a Graduate in Engineering/ Technology from any AICTE approved institute with at least 50% marks and (45% for SC/ST/OBC candidates of M.P.) of minimum of four years duration after passing 10+2 system or equivalent.

M.B.A. (F.T. 2 Years)

A candidate seeking admission to MBA and Allied Courses should be a Graduate with at least 50% marks and 45% marks in case of SC, ST, OBC (excluding Creamy Layer) category of Madhya Pradesh in aggregate of any UGC/AICTE approved Indian University or from a foreign University recognized by Association of Indian Universities (AIU) or institute recognized by the concerned University as equivalent thereto. The Bachelor's degree must be of minimum three years duration after passing Higher Secondary (10+2) examination of (10+2) system or equivalent

(Seats are reserved for SC/ST/OBC candidates are per government norms in all above courses)
